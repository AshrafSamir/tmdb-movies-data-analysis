TMDB Movies Dataset¶

This data set contains information about 10,000 movies collected from The Movie Database (TMDb), including user ratings and revenue. we are here to provide helpful questions and its answers.

Questions¶

  1. Which genres are most popular from year to year?
  2. What kinds of properties are associated with movies that have high revenues?
  3. What movies couldn't cover its budget(revenue = 0)?
  4. What is the hieghest release genres?

  • We found the most popular genres in each year then we got the best from them all and we ranked them based on their counts from year to year Adventure 31 Action 28 Science Fiction 20 Thriller 20 Drama 14 Family 11 Fantasy 10 Animation 8 Crime 7 Comedy 6 Mystery 3 Romance 3 Horror 3 Music 2
  • budget and runtime have higher correlation with revenue than runtime

  • The hieghest release genres were Drame, Comedy, Thriller, Action, Romance, Horror, Adventure, Crime etc..
## Limitations
  • Dropping missing or Null values from variables of our interest might skew our analysis and could show unintentional bias towards the relationship being analyzed. etc.
  • We couldn't find strong correlation with revenue due to the lack of data that have high revenue and also the outliers
  • We have limited analysis with our datset, because we worked only with a few of the given features
